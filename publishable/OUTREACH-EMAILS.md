# Endorsement-request emails (drafts)

How to use:
- **Start the arXiv submission first** so arXiv issues you an endorsement **code** — paste it into
  `[CODE]` below. (New authors get this automatically; the endorser clicks the link.)
- **Attach the compiled PDF** (`paper1-...pdf`).
- **Replace** `[your email]` and `[your ORCID]`.
- **One or two at a time, never CC.** Personalize the tailored line for each recipient.
- Keep the subject specific; keep the body short (they're busy).
- Endorsement only vouches the paper fits the category — it is *not* a referee report; say so, it
  lowers the bar for a "yes."

**Subject:** arXiv endorsement request (quant-ph): non-Markovian gravitational decoherence from indivisible processes

Dear Professor Barandes,

I am an independent researcher (no academic affiliation) writing to ask whether you would consider
endorsing a short paper of mine for arXiv (quant-ph). An endorsement here would only confirm the
paper is appropriate for the category — not that you agree with it.

The paper builds directly on your indivisible-stochastic-processes framework. It asks what
gravitational (Diósi–Penrose) decoherence looks like if the underlying process is genuinely
non-divisible: dropping the divisibility/white-noise assumption behind the DP exponential, the
coherence acquires a non-exponential, Gaussian (quadratic) short-time onset at the same energy
scale τ_G = ℏ/E_G. That difference is a fit-robust experimental signature — the onset *shape*,
plus E_G-scaling, plus a non-Gaussian fingerprint (a kurtosis term, up to coherence revivals) that
separates discrete-record dynamics from generic Gaussian colored noise — distinguishing it from strict
Markovian DP/CSL and from colored CSL.

I treat ISP as the *motivation* for finite memory rather than claiming a first-principles derivation,
but an explicit division-event (renewal-reset) model does ground the exponential kernel and pin τ_c in
your division-event structure — which may interest you. I state plainly what is assumed (the DP energy
scale), what is generic (the quadratic onset), and what the indivisible structure supplies (the finite
memory, and the non-Gaussian fingerprint).

(https://arxiv.org/auth/endorse?x=MRT74X). And if you happen to have any reaction to whether the
