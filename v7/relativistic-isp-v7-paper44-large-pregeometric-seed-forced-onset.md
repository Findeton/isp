## 0. Question

Paper XLIII proved a scoped equivalence:

$$
\boxed{
\text{proper finite spacetime onset}
\quad\Longleftrightarrow_{\rm scoped}\quad
\text{stable non-reconstructive geometric compression of no-silent residue}.
}
$$

But it did not prove that such a route is forced from an initial condition.

A single record has no interval, no overlap, no transport loop, no center
comparison, no scale anchor, and no persistent residue.  It can be a primitive
existence seed, but it cannot force geometric compression by itself.

The better question is:

$$
\boxed{
\text{is there a large pre-geometric seed class }S_0
\text{ such that spacetime onset is forced?}
}
$$

This paper investigates that question.

If a large pre-geometric seed satisfies recurrent geometric residue,
