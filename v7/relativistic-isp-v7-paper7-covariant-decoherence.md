(The GDC *finite-energy* half additionally needs their normal-ordering prescription, which SHARD does not carry; only the microcausality half is inherited.) **(ii) The colored noise is structural, not ad hoc. [STRUCTURAL]** SHARD does not *derive* the kernel — its weight-0 odometer content is the wrong type to be a two-point function, and the positive-type quartic template is one admissible profile among many. ## 1. Introduction: the covariance question

Paper I derived the single-chain click-law: the seal is an orthogonal projection, the content `χ` a forced odometer, the survival law `exp(−κχ)` in the divisible regime, with a free inter-seal coherence profile and one free scale. At coarse grain this is a dynamical-collapse model in the Ghirardi–Rimini–Weber / continuous-spontaneous-localization family — a stochastic norm-preserving reduction with a locality kernel. The thermodynamic papers (v6 X/XI) showed its Gaussian decoherence onset is the same Tier-A law under a content reparametrization, not a separate signature.

Dynamical-collapse models have a standing difficulty with special relativity. A naive instantaneous reduction is frame-dependent and can leak superluminal signalling; making collapse relativistic has been the field's central open problem since Ghirardi. Two layers must be distinguished. The **decoherence / master-equation layer** governs how mixed states and correlation functions evolve — here relativity demands *microcausality*: spacelike-separated operations commute, and the Tomonaga–Schwinger integrability condition `[H(x), H(y)] = 0` holds for spacelike `x, y`. The **statevector / beable layer** governs an objective, frame-independent collapsing state — here relativity demands a consistent collapsing wavefunctional across all Cauchy surfaces. The two layers have very different status in 2025, and the contribution of this paper is to place the SHARD click-law precisely against each, claiming only what the records support.

The discipline is pre-geometric throughout: the seal, its content, and its kernel are record-internal objects (a projection, a KL number, a function of the substrate's own interval invariant); spacetime and the Lorentz group enter only as the *emergent* structure the records are tested against, never as a derivation input. The causal order against which microcausality is stated is the frame-invariant order of the record substrate (v6 paper 2), and the discreteness that supplies the ultraviolet cutoff is the causal-set / flash structure that covariantizes the kinematics.

## 2. The seal is the right kind of object: a self-adjoint Lorentz scalar [SUPPLIED]

The 2025 microcausality theorem requires the collapse-driving operator to be a self-adjoint Lorentz *scalar* — an operator like `Q = ½ α :φ²:`, transforming trivially under the (emergent) Lorentz group and equal to its own adjoint. SHARD's seal meets the algebraic half of this outright. Receipt `c1` confirms the seal `E` is self-adjoint (`E = E*`), so the reflection `q = 2E − 1` is a self-adjoint involution — a scalar seal — and the content `χ = D(P_AB ‖ P_BA)` is a weight-0 KL *number*, invariant under the substrate's internal relabelings. Both the seal and its content are scalar objects under the screen automorphisms; the seal is the right *kind* of object for the theorem.

The caveat is explicit and is carried forward as the §4 premise: "scalar" here is established under the substrate's *internal* automorphisms. Whether it is a scalar under the *emergent* Lorentz action is not an algebraic fact but an emergence statement — it holds if the coarse-graining that produces the Lorentz group acts on the seal as a scalar. This is part of the one conditional premise, not an independent gap.

## 4. Locality is the load-bearing gap, and the inheritance is conditional [INHERITED / CONDITIONAL]

With the scalar seal (§2) and the Lorentz-invariant positive-type noise (§3) in hand, the 2025 all-orders microcausality recursion needs one further input: the collapse-driving density must be **point-local**. This is exactly where SHARD's primitive differs from the theorem's hypothesis — the record primitive is a finite holonomy *diamond*, a region with a collar, not a point. Locality is therefore the one *new* load-bearing gap (the emergent-Lorentz-scalar property of §2 is the pre-existing covariance-frontier premise, carried forward, not an independent assumption introduced here), and it is what stands between the SHARD click-law and inherited covariance at this layer.

Receipt `c1` makes the inheritance condition operational on a finite causal-lattice operator model. Sites carry a discrete causal partial order (a lattice light-cone, mimicking the emergent causal set). A **point-local** scalar seal density `h(x)` acts only at site `x` (a single-site self-adjoint operator) — the coarse-grained, collar → 0 limit. A **region-smeared** seal density `h_R(x)` acts on a cluster of sites that can straddle a spacelike gap — the diamond with finite collar. Against a spacelike-separated probe `O(z)`:

This operationalizes the **locality ⟺ spacelike-commutation hinge** on which the GDC recursion turns — it is not a re-derivation of that recursion (for a single point-local scalar density the higher orders vanish trivially once the first does; GDC's published all-orders result, nesting distinct multi-point operators against two probes, is what is borrowed). With the hinge met, the decoherence layer of the SHARD click-law **inherits** the 2025 all-orders microcausality result — and with it the spacelike half `[H(x), H(y)] = 0` of Tomonaga–Schwinger integrability — **if and only if** the holonomy diamond coarse-grains, under an emergent Lorentz action, to a **point-local self-adjoint Lorentz-scalar seal density** (collar → 0). The premise is motivated — a coarse-graining that produces a relativistic continuum should send a finite microstructure to a point, and the causal-set / flash discreteness covariantizes the kinematics in just this way — but it is an emergence statement about the diamond → point limit, not a theorem, and it is tagged **[CONDITIONAL]** accordingly. What was an open frontier becomes a single load-bearing hypothesis with a clean failure mode (region-smearing breaks microcausality at first order), which is a genuine reduction of the open surface.

## 5. Colored noise is structural, not ad hoc [STRUCTURAL]

It is tempting to claim more — that SHARD *derives* the noise kernel, beating the literature's free functional choice. It does not, and the paper says so. Receipt `c2` runs the decisive test: to derive the kernel SHARD would have to force a *unique* Lorentz-invariant, positive-type, finite-energy `G(s²)`, and it fails on two independent counts. First, the natural Tier-A object — the odometer content `χ`, or the fractional part `frac(χ/d)` — is a **weight-0 scalar KL number**, the wrong *type* to be a two-point correlation function `G(s²)`; it cannot even be evaluated on the kernel's spacetime-interval domain without importing the very emergent geometry under test. Second, the positive-type quartic template is one admissible profile among many: like the literature's kernel it is *free* within the positive-type, finite-energy class. So the kernel is not derived — SHARD's kernel has the **same status as the literature's "one of many."**

What SHARD *does* supply is structural and worth stating exactly. Receipt `c2` records the literature's required properties: Lorentz invariance, positive-type, and **finite energy** — the last because white noise (`ĝ ≡ 1`) makes the ultraviolet heating integral `∫ q² ĝ(q) dq` **diverge** (infinite energy injection, the standard collapse-model catastrophe). The colored, finite-energy kernel is the field's hardest *ad hoc* repair: it is imposed by hand precisely to avoid this divergence. In SHARD it is not imposed — the records' principled discreteness gives a built-in ultraviolet cutoff, so the noise being **colored rather than white is forced by construction**, and the infinite-heating catastrophe cannot arise. The honest claim is therefore "the *coloring* of the noise is structural, not ad hoc" — a real, smaller positive result — and **not** "the kernel is derived."

## 6. The objective interacting-field statevector is the field's shared wall [WALL]

The statevector layer is where the click-law, and every objective-collapse program, meets a harder obstruction. A frame-independent, objectively collapsing *statevector* for an **interacting** quantum field — with variable particle number — requires a consistent collapsing wavefunctional defined across all Cauchy surfaces, which in turn requires the field's Hilbert spaces on different surfaces to be unitarily equivalent. Generic interacting QFT lacks this — it is a **Haag-type obstruction** (unitary inequivalence of interacting field representations; relatedly, the Tomonaga–Schwinger equation's validity in quantum field theory is disproven in dimension `> 2`). Fedida (2025) is instructive precisely here: working in the Tomonaga–Schwinger picture, it *assumes* unitarily equivalent Hilbert spaces along Cauchy surfaces as a precondition and from them derives positive Einstein-causality results (state-independent spacelike commutation, no-signalling) — corroborating the §4 spacelike-microcausality side — while *acknowledging*, citing the standard QFT facts, that the precondition fails generically. The wall is thus the long-known Haag obstruction, not a new result; and it is a wall of the **whole field**, not of SHARD: it blocks relativistic GRW-flash (Tumulka), CSL, and any objective-statevector collapse program equally.

The residue factors cleanly. Write the remaining obstruction as `B = B1 × B2`, where `B1` asks whether an interaction-local Tomonaga–Schwinger field unitary exists at all, and `B2` asks whether collapse can be inserted given `B1`. Every factor except `B1` is discharged by the preceding sections — `B2` modulo the same §4 point-local emergence premise that conditions the microcausality inheritance — so the two-layer status is clean: `B1` is the **unconditional shared Haag wall**, and `B2` holds **conditional on the one premise**. SHARD is therefore **no worse off** than the rest of the field at the statevector layer, and strictly better off at the decoherence layer (it supplies the scalar seal and the colored kernel that the generic model must postulate). Two further disciplines are observed. SHARD stays strictly **stochastic-linear**: it does not add a deterministic nonlinear term to the evolution, which would fall foul of the deterministic-nonlinear signalling no-go — the records' nonlinearity lives entirely in the *stochastic* reduction, on the safe side. And the beable claim is scoped to what the substrate actually carries: the records realize a relativistic *flash / event* ontology (the seals are the flashes), for which covariant constructions are known in the free / distinguishable case (Tumulka), not an objective interacting-field wavefunctional, which runs into `B1`.
