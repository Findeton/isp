## Abstract

The sealed-record program (SHARD) has, to date, supplied the *thermodynamics* of records — the Born rule, the entropy/area relations, the gravitational equation of state, the scale no-go — by working with *given* record configurations and *assuming* the pieces of dynamics it needed (an arrow, a local equilibrium, a postulated seal hazard). This paper takes the next step: it asks for the **click-law** itself — the whole-history rule that fixes *when, where, and how a record commits* — and derives it from first principles, with one essential discipline. SHARD is **pre-geometric**: space and time are emergent, so the fundamental click-law must be stated at the **record level alone**, with no spacetime, metric, light cone, or interval `s²`; the geometric and thermodynamic constraints are *conditional consequences* of an emergent-spacetime regime, not axioms. We separate the law into two tiers. **Tier A (fundamental, pre-geometric):** the seal `E` is forced to be an **orthogonal projection** (`E=E*=E²`, by norm-preservation — *not* by `q²=1`, which an oblique idempotent also satisfies) under the `q=2` screen calculus; the intrinsic content `χ = D(P_AB‖P_BA)` is a **forced odometer** — `dχ ≥ 0` unconditionally (Gibbs/arrow-positivity), and concatenation-additive as a theorem of arrow-positivity *given* `χ` is the accumulated entropy production over a Markov-presentable commit order; and self-consistency under refinement then forces the survival law by *regime*. In the **divisible (dense-seal)** regime the Cauchy multiplicative equation `S(χ₁+χ₂)=S(χ₁)S(χ₂)` gives the unique `S(χ) = exp(−κχ)` (constant hazard per unit content; uniqueness under monotonicity + measurability), with `κ ≥ 0` (no revival) the corollary that a revival is a non-composing point — *not a seal*. In the genuinely-**indivisible (sparse-seal)** regime, composition holds only on the seal lattice and forces a **geometric-progression skeleton** `S(nd)=S(d)ⁿ` (geometric in the *sequence* sense, not spacetime), while the **inter-seal coherence profile and the seal spacing remain free** — and that free profile *is* the genuine-quantum content the dense limit annihilates, with the dense exponential recovered (for monotone inter-seal continuations) exactly as the spacing `d→0`. The content `χ` is a weight-zero, record-intrinsic Kullback–Leibler number; the single conversion **κ** (content → commit-order rate) is the **one free scale** — turning a weight-zero number into a rate requires one external unit the records cannot carry — *plausibly* the same scale the program's gravitational no-go isolates (by structural analogy, §3.4). So the fundamental click-law is **forced up to one calibration** — the best possible (`unique-modulo-one-no-go-scale`) Branch-A outcome for the law's *shape*. **Tier B (conditional):** *if* the records coarse-grain into a spacetime satisfying the program's regularity condition, *then* the content lays down along an emergent parameter as a profile `χ(T)`, and the thermodynamic results follow as the spacetime limit — including the observation that the exponential and Gaussian-onset decoherence curves are the *same* Tier-A law `exp(−κχ)` read through linear vs. quadratic `χ(T)`. We are explicit that the multiplicativity is *along the refinement (sequential) direction* and does **not** forbid the non-local *correlation* between parallel record-chains that entanglement requires — that correlation is a separate Tier-A structure, here left open. The honest residue is now sharp: what remains open is (i) the *magnitude* of the content increment `dχ` (its structure — additivity and sign — is forced), (ii) the entanglement correlation, and (iii) the inter-seal quantum profile and the seal spacing (the sparse *skeleton* is forced) — i.e. whether gravitational sealing realizes that free quantum content or is a continuous (classical) shadow.

## 1. The problem, and why it must be pre-geometric

A record is a *sealed finite holonomy diamond* (the primitive of SHARD): a self-contained finite parcel `D = (Ω_D, μ_D, screens, collar, transports)` whose physical content is the closed exchange-defect holonomy `Δ_AB = T_B T_A − T_A T_B`, and which becomes an **event** when a count-symmetric idempotent proposition `E` commits. The thermodynamics of *given* such configurations is established; what is missing is the **click-law** — the whole-history rule `P^hist` for *when/where/how* a new record commits. This is the dynamical engine. We seek to derive it.

Two constraints set the method. First, the **do-delete no-go**: two distinct lower-level commitment rules can produce the *same* observed history, so the click-law is **not an observational statistic** — it cannot be read from data and must be pinned by a *self-consistency* principle. Second, and decisively, **SHARD is pre-geometric**: time is the commit order, space and the metric are emergent, so the fundamental law must be written with **no spacetime input** — no Lorentz frame, no light cone, no interval `s²`, no proper time. Constraints that presuppose geometry (microcausality, Lorentz invariance, a local kernel `f(s²)`, the thermodynamic equilibrium premise) are therefore *not* axioms of the click-law; they are **conditional consequences** in an emergent-spacetime regime. This separates the law into two tiers, and isolating them is what makes the fundamental law tractable: the hard relativistic-localization problem is quarantined into Tier B as an *emergence* question, not a constraint on the fundamental rule.

- **Tier A** — the fundamental, pre-geometric click-law, on records alone.
- **Tier B** — the conditional regime: *if* a spacetime emerges (under the program's regularity condition), *then* the geometric/thermodynamic structure follows.

§2–§3 derive Tier A. §4 states the Tier-B conditional. §5 is the honest residue.

## 3. Tier A — the fundamental click-law

### 3.1 The seal is an orthogonal projection. [FORCED]

The `q=2` weight calculus (Born composition, Banach–Lamperti) forces the retained-holonomy screen to be a **2-norm (Hilbert) space** with *norm-preserving* transports. A count-symmetric idempotent readout `E²=E` that preserves the screen norm cannot be oblique: in a 2-norm an idempotent is norm-non-increasing iff it is **self-adjoint**. Hence **norm-preservation** forces the seal to be an **orthogonal projection**, `E²=E=E*` (the discriminating property is self-adjointness — an *oblique* idempotent has `q²=1` too but is *not* screen-norm-preserving; receipt `v7/code/f1_born_projection_q2.py`), and `q = 2E−1` is then a self-adjoint **reflection**, `q²=1`. This is purely algebraic — no geometry. [FORCED]

### 3.2 The seal-law shape: a forced exponential (divisible) and a forced power-law lattice skeleton (indivisible). [FORCED]

A **seal is a refinement point of the commit order** — a point where, in the Gell-Mann–Hartle bridge (the projector sequence ordered by the **commit order**, never by a metric time), the decoherence functional `D(α,α′)` diagonalizes and the pointer histories compose (Chapman–Kolmogorov). Given the projective seal-history model, this biconditional is **forced at the kinematic level as an algebraic identity**: the Chapman–Kolmogorov defect *equals* the interference cross-term of `D` entrywise (`Σ|·|²` vs `|Σ·|²`; receipt `f3d_foundational_supports.py`, gap `6.6×10⁻¹⁴²`), and the model's two ingredients — `Γ=|U|²` and orthogonal projective pointers — are themselves Tier-A-forced by §3.1. So **all forcing below is relative to this *itself-forced* bridge.** What the bridge does *not* settle is the **intrinsic supply** of seals, which splits into (a) the seal rate/shape — the `κ` and the magnitude of `dχ`, Paper 1's own Tier-A open frontier (§3.4, §5.1) — and (b) the seal *placement* / which `E` commits / sparse-vs-dense, a Tier-B gravitational computation deferred to the companion Level-3 functor; neither is presupposed in the forcing chain. Self-consistency under refinement is the demand that **inserting an intermediate seal must compose**; one premise it needs is a *theorem*, not a separate assumption, and we split by regime.

**The content is a forced odometer. [FORCED — `dχ≥0` unconditional; additivity given the corpus definition `χ`:=accumulated `σ`]** `χ` is additive under *concatenation along the commit order*, `χ(seg₁ then seg₂) = χ(seg₁) + χ(seg₂)`, with a non-negative increment `dχ ≥ 0`. The `dχ ≥ 0` half is **unconditional** (`dχ = σ_step = D(P_AB‖P_BA) ≥ 0` by Gibbs/Arrow-Positivity, equality only at a detailed-balance step, which is eventless and so *not a seal*). The additivity half is a **theorem of arrow-positivity** given the corpus definition that `χ` is the accumulated entropy production `σ` (§2): on the eventless inter-seal collar the trajectory action `A[ω] = \log(dP_fwd/dP_rev)` *telescopes* into a sum of per-step increments and concatenation-additivity holds for *any* start (receipt `f3c_sequential_odometer.py`: `A[seg₁→seg₂] − A[seg₁] − A[seg₂] = 7.7×10⁻¹²¹` on a *driven* chain, no detailed balance). The collar's **Markov-presentability** is itself *forced* within paper10's standing arena — an eventless collar is detailed-balance reversible (T3), and a reversible primitive collar transport has a finite primitive Markov presentation (T4) — *not* a separate assumption; and it is *consistent* with the indivisibility (non-Markovianity) of the **seal order**, since the two are properties of different objects: the collar's content-transport telescopes (gap `0.0`), while the seal order's coherent one-time law `Γ=|U|²` fails to compose across an *unsealed* interval (receipt `f3d`, CK gap `0.329`) and composes only at a seal — *Markov where the process is order-blind, non-Markov where it commits*; the sole residue is paper10's own inherited open (infinite-memory collars). This sequential additivity is a *different* composition from the parallel/tensor cocycle `A_{D₁×D₂}=A_{D₁}+A_{D₂}` (which holds for *parallel* diamonds — receipt gap `0.0`, machine-zero — but is the wrong additivity here). So the odometer's *structure* is forced; only the *magnitude* of each `dχ` (and the one scale `κ`) is the separate open question of §5.1.

**Divisible (dense-seal) regime — forced exponential.** If refinement points are **dense in `χ`** (continuous sealing), *every* insertion composes and survival is multiplicative for **all** splits,

  **`S(χ₁ + χ₂) = S(χ₁)·S(χ₂)`**   (the Cauchy multiplicative equation),

whose unique solution under `S(0)=1`, monotonicity, and measurability (the Aczél conditions, which exclude the non-measurable Hamel pathologies) is

  **`S(χ) = exp(−κ χ)`,   constant hazard per unit content,  `λ(χ) = κ`.**

### 3.3 No revival. [DERIVED, corollary of §3.2]

A committed record cannot un-commit, and that is all the no-revival result ever was. [DERIVED, corollary]

So the fundamental click-law is **forced up to exactly one calibration** — the best possible Branch-A outcome for the law's shape, `unique-modulo-one-no-go-scale`. The free `κ` here is *plausibly* the same missing scale the gravitational no-go identifies — both being the unique content→rate conversion a weight-zero record number cannot fix; whether they are literally the same number is argued by this structural analogy, not proven here. ### 3.5 Entanglement: the guardrail. [OPEN, Tier-A]

The multiplicativity `S(χ₁+χ₂)=S(χ₁)S(χ₂)` is **along the refinement (sequential) direction** — the composition of stretches of a *single* record chain. It must **not** be read as "independent ⇒ uncorrelated." The sequential seal-law neither requires nor forbids it. The genuine Tier-A obligation is the program's Bell constraint — the joint sealing of correlated chains must **keep parameter-independence (no signaling) while dropping outcome-independence (correlated outcomes)** — and whether the joint click-law carrying this correlation is itself forced (or merely constrained) by self-consistency is **not settled here**. We flag explicitly that locality must never be imposed on this Tier-A correlation structure; only the *emergent dynamics* (Tier B) is local, and only as no-signaling. [OPEN, Tier-A]

## 6. What this paper does and does not claim

It **claims**, with high-precision receipts: that the seal is forced to be an orthogonal projection (§3.1); that the intrinsic content is a **forced odometer** — `dχ≥0` unconditionally, and sequentially additive as a theorem of arrow-positivity given `χ`:=accumulated `σ` over a Markov-presentable commit order (§3.2); that — *in the divisible (dense-seal) regime, under monotonicity + measurability* — self-consistency forces the survival law to the unique `S(χ)=exp(−κχ)`, with `p=1` the only refinement-consistent exponent, while in the **indivisible (sparse) regime the seal-lattice skeleton is forced geometric** `S(nd)=S(d)^n` with the inter-seal profile and spacing free, and the dense exponential is the `d→0` limit (§3.2); that no-revival is the corollary that a non-composing point is not a seal (§3.3); that the law is forced up to exactly one calibration `κ`, *plausibly* the same scale the gravitational no-go isolates (§3.4); and that the exponential and Gaussian decoherence onsets are the same Tier-A law under reparametrization, with the thermodynamics recovered as the conditional spacetime limit (§4).

It does **not** claim: a derivation of the *magnitude* of `dχ` — only the odometer's *structure* (additivity + sign) is forced, not the size of each increment (open, §5.1); a derivation of the entanglement correlation (open, §5.2); that the inter-seal quantum profile or the seal spacing is fixed, nor that gravitational sealing is in fact sparse rather than continuous — the sparse *skeleton* is forced, but the profile, the spacing, and the dense-vs-sparse realization are open (§5.3); a value for `κ` (provably one free scale); the *literal* identity of `κ` with the gravitational scale (argued by analogy, §3.4); or any geometric/relativistic content as an *axiom* — the equation of state and the scale no-go are claimed only as the *conditional* spacetime limit, while Lorentz invariance and microcausality are *not* claimed at all (§4). The law delivers *when* (`λ=κ`) and *how* (`E` a projection); *where* (a locality kernel) is only an emergent Tier-B notion, not a Tier-A output. Nothing here presupposes space, time, a metric, or `s²`.

The one-line summary: **in the divisible (dense-seal) regime, self-consistency under refinement forces the pre-geometric record click-law to a constant hazard per unit holonomy content, `S(χ)=exp(−κχ)`, unique up to the single calibration `κ` the program proved must remain free; the genuinely-indivisible (sparse) law and the content's own supply are the sharp open frontier, and the thermodynamics is the emergent-spacetime limit.**
