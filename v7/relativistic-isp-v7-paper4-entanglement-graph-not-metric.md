## Abstract

This paper takes the *constructive* direction — geometry **from** entanglement (Van Raamsdonk / Ryu–Takayanagi / ER=EPR), not the reverse — and reports a sharp two-part result, with the joint sealing dynamics closed in status along the way. **First, the joint click-law on parallel entangled chains is forced-skeleton, free-complement.** The multiplicative survival *shape* `S = exp(−κχ)` transfers verbatim from one chain to the joint scalar argument (the Cauchy/refinement-multiplicativity step is content-blind), but the *additivity* of the joint content `X = χ_A + χ_B` is **not** forced: the joint commit-order refinement telescopes exactly (gap `0`) while leaving a non-additive mutual term `χ_AB` untouched — it is provably *blind* to entanglement, because additivity is equivalent to the *separate* `A|B`-cut factorization the sequential argument never invokes. So entanglement is **permitted, never forced, never forbidden**: the *dynamical* entangling content is the non-additive functional `χ_AB = I_σ` (the joint-odometer interaction information / factorization defect, `S_AB/(S_A S_B) = exp(−κχ_AB)` exactly), a genuine **free** Tier-A input — two-sided in sign, a continuum at fixed marginals — constrained *only* by no-signaling (parameter-independence) and the Bell/Tsirelson envelope (`CHSH ≤ 2√2` saturable, the PR-box `4` excluded). It vanishes *when* the joint ledger factorizes (a *necessary*, not sufficient, condition — being signed, `I_σ` can also cancel at special correlated kernels, `p4a` exhibiting `I_σ ≈ 0` at factorization-defect `0.09`); only the *non-negative* kinematic measure `E_cl` (the nearest-product KL; `E_cl(Bell) = ln 2`) vanishes *iff* factorized. The signed `I_σ` and the non-negative `E_cl` are distinct functionals sharing only the factorized ⟹ zero direction, not one number. Survival-multiplicativity along the sealing axis is orthogonal to outcome-factorization (OI) on the transverse axis, so the forced skeleton *permits* a full OI-violating, no-signaling, Tsirelson-saturating correlation — realizing the keep-PI/drop-OI Bell address at the click-law level. This upgrades Paper I's `[OPEN]` to *"shape forced, no-signaling the only extra constraint, entanglement content free."* **Second, that entanglement sources the record graph but provably not its metric.** The entanglement content (the mutual information `I(i:j)`, and indeed the whole joint KL content) is **weight-0** (a pure KL number, gauge-invariant under the record-length rescaling), so it sources weight-0 structure: **connectivity/topology** (the Van Raamsdonk pinch — zero mutual information ⇒ no edge ⇒ disconnected component) and **the connectivity graph's hop-metric up to the one overall scale** (the unweighted graph's integer shortest-path distances, whose ratios `d_ij/d_kl` are invariant under `l_step → μ l_step`; only the single multiplicative unit `l_step` is free). But the absolute **distance/metric is weight +1** (a length `= #hops × l_step`), and by the same grading-homomorphism trichotomy that forbids Newton's `G` (Paper III) and the spacing `d`, a weight-0 record functional can never supply the one absolute `l_step`. So **entanglement → spacetime is exactly half-open: connectivity and the hop-metric up to one scale yes (weight-0, non-circular), absolute metric no — the Newton-constant no-go a third time.** A circularity audit shows that every existing "geometry from entanglement" in the corpus runs the *opposite* (Jacobson) way — geometry assumed, entanglement-area read off — and names the one trap a clean construction must avoid (scoring against true coordinates from a metric-generated sprinkling). The full bulk-emergence (an absolute metric, the RT area-*coefficient*, an ER=EPR wormhole *length*) is no-go-blocked for the scale and premature for the rest; the entanglement *content* of the gravitational record process remains the open Tier-A obligation. Pre-geometric throughout: every quantity is a record-internal probability/KL number — the result is precisely that the records yield a graph and its hop-metric up to one scale, but not an absolute geometry.

## 1. Introduction: the constructive direction, and the two halves

Papers I–III and V worked "downward" — forcing the click-law's shape, bounding the spacing, proving the scale and the gravitational strength are not the records' to fix. This paper works the other way: can the Tier-A *entanglement correlation* — a pre-geometric record structure — **source** emergent geometry, in the Van Raamsdonk sense (reduce entanglement and spacetime pinches off), without smuggling in a background metric?

The honest answer splits in two, and the split is the program's own no-go. We first settle the **dynamics** — is the joint sealing law on parallel entangled chains forced (§3)? — because without it "entanglement" has no derived law to source anything. Then we settle the **geometry** — what can a weight-0 correlation actually build (§4–5)? The discipline throughout (Paper I's two-tier guardrail): entanglement is a Tier-A correlation, needs no spacetime, and the construction must define the correlation *before* any metric. The weight grading is what keeps that honest: stay weight-0 and the construction is legitimate; smuggle a length and it is circular.

## 2. The Tier-A entanglement structure SHARD already has

Entanglement is representable at the record level with no geometric input. The corpus carries a closed-form, gauge-invariant, spacetime-free bipartite correlation `E_cl(A:B)` [21] — the column-averaged Kullback–Leibler divergence of the joint transition law `Γ_AB` from its nearest *product* law — defined entirely on `Γ` (non-negative entries, unit column sums); it is non-negative, gauge-invariant, locally monotone, recovers the Bell-state value `E_cl = ln 2` and the CHSH circuit, and vanishes exactly on a product law. The ontology is *finite same-process nonfactorization* [5']: a joint interface is entangled iff its joint constraint ledger cannot be replaced by independent local ledgers plus shared classical records without changing the joint statistics. And the Bell address is fixed [14]: any single-history theory reproducing CHSH keeps parameter-independence and no-signaling, keeps single outcomes, and drops *outcome*-independence (the Jarrett decomposition `LC = PI ∧ OI`). So the "long connection between distant records" is a genuine pre-geometric object — a record correlation with `PI`-yes/`OI`-no — available before any metric. What was open (Paper I §3.5/§5.2) is the *dynamical* question of §3: is the joint law that *seals* this correlation forced?

## 3. The joint click-law: forced skeleton, free complement [FORCED + FREE]

Single-chain sealing was forced to `S(χ) = exp(−κχ)` by refinement-multiplicativity `S(χ₁+χ₂) = S(χ₁)S(χ₂)` (Paper I §3.2). On two parallel chains with a joint constraint ledger, the joint odometer decomposes **exactly** over the product state space:

  `σ_AB = D(P_AB‖P_BA) = σ_A + σ_B + I_σ`,

with `σ_A, σ_B` the coarse-grained marginal odometers and `I_σ` the mutual term. A product (factorized) ledger gives `I_σ = 0` exactly (gap `5×10⁻¹⁴¹`) — additive, `S_AB = S_A S_B`, *no* entanglement; a correlated ledger gives `I_σ ≠ 0` (e.g. `−0.0712`) — non-additive, entanglement (`p4a`).

**The skeleton is forced; the additivity is not.** Two refinement directions must be distinguished. The *sequential* one (concatenation along the joint commit order) is the only one the single-chain Cauchy argument uses — and it **telescopes exactly for the correlated joint odometer (gap `0`) while `I_σ = −0.0712 ≠ 0`**: it is provably *blind* to the mutual term. Additivity `X = χ_A + χ_B` is equivalent to the *separate* `A|B`-cut (parallel) factorization, which the sequential argument never invokes. So the exponential *shape* on the scalar joint argument is forced exactly as on one chain — in the divisible/dense-seal regime, under the same single-channel + stationarity premises Paper I §3.2 names (a genuinely-sparse joint regime inherits Paper I's geometric-progression skeleton instead) — Cauchy residual `0`, `p=1` only, a single `κ`, the product limit forcing `exp` for any stationary per-step law. The point is that the single-chain premise — *survival is a function of the accumulated content alone, one scalar* — **is** satisfied jointly for `X = σ_AB` (itself one number), so the shape transfers; the genuinely **new degree of freedom** is in *how* that scalar **decomposes** across the `A|B` cut (`X = χ_A + χ_B + χ_AB`), which the single-chain law never constrains. The machine-verified identity is

  `S_AB / (S_A · S_B) = exp(−κ · χ_AB) = exp(−κ · I_σ)`  (gap `0`, `p4a`):

the multiplicative factorization defect *is* the exponential of the mutual content.

**The complement is the entanglement, and it is free.** The *dynamical* entangling content — the term that actually enters the survival law — is `χ_AB = I_σ`, the joint-odometer interaction information; it is the **signed factorization defect** that drives `S_AB/(S_A S_B) = exp(−κ χ_AB)`. It vanishes *when* the joint ledger factorizes — a **necessary, not sufficient** condition: being a *difference* of entropy productions, `I_σ` can also cancel at a genuinely correlated kernel (`p4a` exhibits a convex mixture with `I_σ = 0` at factorization-defect `≈ 0.09`), so `{factorized} ⊊ {I_σ = 0}`. It is **free** given the marginals (a continuum of distinct nonzero values at fixed marginals, `χ_A` spread `10⁻¹⁴¹`) and **two-sided** in sign — a conditional-mutual-information-type quantity, *not* a static `MI ≥ 0` (e.g. `−0.0712` for a correlated ledger, `+0.0182` for a no-signaling one). It shares only the **factorized ⟹ zero direction** (a zero-set *containment*, not equality) with two other nonfactorization quantities — the *kinematic* measure `E_cl` (the *non-negative* nearest-product KL of `Γ_AB`; `E_cl(Bell) = ln 2`, `E_cl(product) = 0`) and the OI-factorization defect; of these, only the **non-negative `E_cl` vanishes *iff*** the ledger factorizes (it is `> 0` on every correlated ledger, the rigorous entanglement measure). They are **distinct functionals**, not one number — the signed entropy-production `I_σ` versus the non-negative outcome-statistics `E_cl`, computed in different framings (`p4a` vs `nonadditive_entangling_complement`), coinciding only in special regimes. (That `I_σ` can vanish on a correlated ledger only makes the complement *freer*, not more constrained.) The *only* extra obligation on `χ_AB` is the no-signaling guardrail: a marginal-preserving correlated ledger has parameter-independence exactly (`PI` gap `10⁻¹⁴¹`) while `I_σ ≠ 0`. The complement becomes forced/unique only as a functional of the *full* bipartite kernel (the nearest-product reference is the unique minimizer).

**The forced skeleton permits OI-violation.** Survival-multiplicativity along the sealing axis is orthogonal to factorization (OI) of the joint outcome law on the transverse axis: the forced skeleton supports a full OI-violating, parameter-independent, no-signaling, Tsirelson-*saturating* (`CHSH = 2√2`) correlation, with the PR-box (`CHSH = 4`) excluded (`f5`). Entanglement is *compatible with and permitted by* the forced dynamics. This upgrades Paper I §5.2 from bare `[OPEN]` to the sharp statement: **the entanglement dynamics is shape-determined but content-open — the entangling content `χ_AB` is a free Tier-A input, on the same footing as `κ` and the increment magnitude, constrained only by no-signaling and Tsirelson.**

## 4. The connectivity/metric weight-split: half-open [NO-GO on the metric]

Now the geometry. The entanglement content — the mutual information `I(i:j)`, and indeed the whole joint KL content `D(P_AB‖P_BA)` — is **weight-0**: a pure KL number in nats, gauge-invariant under the record-length rescaling `l → μl` (`p4b`). A graph **distance/metric** is **weight +1**: a length `= #hops × l_step`, scaling as `μ`. By the grading-homomorphism trichotomy of Paper III (the weight is an additive grading; a weight-0 record functional is either a ratio-of-twins, scale-blind, or a Tier-A amplitude — never a weight +1 object), the records **cannot supply the one absolute `l_step`** a metric needs. Therefore:

  **entanglement can source weight-0 structure (connectivity, and the graph's hop-metric up to one overall scale) but provably *not* the absolute metric (weight +1).** **[FORCED]**

This is the **third appearance of the same wall**: the missing absolute `l_step` blocks the spacing `d` (Paper II–III), Newton's `G` (Paper 57), and now the emergent metric. The Van Raamsdonk direction is exactly **half-open** — topology yes, distance no — blocked by the identical no-go that kills `G`. (The matter scale of Paper V is the same gate seen on the strength axis.)

## 5. What entanglement *does* source: the pinch and the hop-metric [PERMITTED]

The weight-0 half is real and non-circular. **Connectivity:** with edges defined by mutual information, an entangled pair `I(A:B) > 0` is an edge and a product pair `I = 0` is *no* edge — so reducing the entanglement between two regions disconnects them (the Van Raamsdonk pinch), as a purely weight-0 statement (`p4b`: a three-node graph with `A–B` entangled and `C` product pinches `C` off). **The hop-metric up to one overall scale:** the weight-0 connectivity graph carries integer shortest-path (hop) distances; the physical metric is `hop-distance × l_step`, and under the one physical scale freedom `l_step → μ l_step` the distance **ratios** `d_ij/d_kl` are invariant while absolute lengths scale by `μ` (`p4b`, ratios invariant to `10⁻¹⁰⁰`). So the records deliver the **connectivity graph's hop-metric up to the one overall scale** — the relative hop-distances, missing only the single multiplicative unit `l_step`. This is genuine and weight-0 but **coarse**: it is *not* a continuous Riemannian similarity class (nothing beyond the unweighted graph is derived; no curvature is exhibited), and *not* a `−log I` "conformal" claim — a `−log I` edge length instead carries an additive zero-point gauge under `I → s I`, fixing distance *differences*, not the ratios `d_ij/d_kl` (which are *not* invariant there), and the full local-Weyl conformal class is *not* what one overall scale delivers (`p4b`). "Entanglement = area" survives only as "= area *up to the one free unit*," the honest weight-0 statement, not the absolute Ryu–Takayanagi coefficient.

## 6. The circularity audit

The constructive claim demands an explicit anti-circularity check, and the program's own weight no-go supplies the clean criterion: a weight-0 construction is legitimate; one that smuggles in `l_step` is circular. Audited against that criterion: **every existing "geometry from entanglement" in the corpus runs the *opposite* (Jacobson) direction** — geometry is assumed and the entanglement–area relation is *read off* as an equation of state (the area-law/Srednicki coefficient uses a flat-space scalar with radii and centrifugal terms; the kinematic-metric construction recovers form and curvature but *off an already-spatial record lattice*, presupposing an ordered space and a fixed adjacency). This is not vicious circularity — no metric is inserted and recovered — but it is induced-gravity-grade: the geometric skeleton is the substrate. The **one genuinely vicious trap to avoid** is an older construction that scores recovered coordinates against the *true simulation coordinates* of a metric-generated sprinkling: recovering a metric that went in is consistency, not emergence. A clean entanglement→geometry construction must therefore (i) define the correlation on `Γ` alone (no Hilbert/tensor arena silently re-imported as the "pre-geometric" stage), (ii) reconstruct only the weight-0 *relative* metric (the ratio class), *blind* (no access to true coordinates), and (iii) never use an already-geometric (weight-bearing) coefficient to "source" geometry.

## 7. What this paper claims and does not

It **claims**, with receipts: that the joint click-law is **forced-skeleton, free-complement** — the exponential survival shape transfers verbatim to the joint scalar argument (Cauchy `0`, `p=1`, one `κ`), while the additivity of the joint content is *not* forced (the sequential refinement telescopes blind to the mutual term, `S_AB/(S_A S_B) = exp(−κ χ_AB)` exactly), so entanglement is permitted with its content a free Tier-A input `χ_AB = I_σ` (the signed interaction information / factorization defect, two-sided, no-signaling/Tsirelson-constrained; `I_σ = 0` is *necessary not sufficient* for factorization — it can cancel on a correlated kernel — whereas the non-negative kinematic `E_cl`, `E_cl(Bell) = ln 2`, vanishes *iff* factorized; the two are distinct functionals) and the forced skeleton supports an OI-violating, Tsirelson-saturating correlation (§3); and that entanglement (weight-0) **sources the record graph's connectivity and its hop-metric up to one overall scale but provably not its absolute metric** (weight +1 — the `l_step` no-go a third time), so entanglement → spacetime is **half-open** (§4–5).

It does **not** claim: an emergent absolute **metric**, the RT minimal-area **coefficient** `1/4G`, or an ER=EPR wormhole **length** (all weight +1, no-go-blocked); a *forcing* of the specific entangling profile `χ_AB` from the parallel-chain record structure (it is a free Tier-A input, the parallel analog of the open `dχ`-supply); the full bulk-emergence (premature — it needs both `χ_AB` for the gravitational ledger *and* the matter sector that `d`, `c_m`, and the masses all wait on); the "particle = stable long entanglement link" ontology (not in the corpus — a separate future program, gated behind the matter sector); or a *derivation* of the Tsirelson ceiling `CHSH ≤ 2√2` and the PR-box exclusion — that is an **external** quantum bound (Tsirelson; Barandes–Hasan–Kagan) which the records here *respect* but do not explain. The open Tier-A obligation is the **entanglement content of the gravitational record process** itself, and whether a *transverse* self-consistency principle (a parallel-chain analog of the one-diamond `KL=Fisher` capacity that fixed the single-chain constants) could convert `χ_AB` from free to forced — untested, the natural next step. The honest one-line summary: **entanglement is permitted by the forced sealing dynamics and sources the record graph and its hop-metric up to one scale non-circularly, but the absolute metric is the Newton-constant no-go again — Van Raamsdonk's connectivity half holds at weight-0; his distance half does not.**
