### 2.2 The proof architecture (the Gibbs–defect route)

Work with the sealed law's density `X = N·P`, dips `S = {X ≤ ½}` with depths `β_s = −log X(s)`, and the dip transform `F(a) = Σ_{s∈S} β_s χ_a(s)`. Four moves.

**(i) The delta bound [one line].** `D_δ = m̂(μ*) = −ln(1 − 1/N) − δ_n < 1/(N−1)` (since `−ln(1−x) < x/(1−x)` and `δ_n > 0`).

**(ii) Low-gap structure with explicit constants.** For any sealed law with `D ≤ c₀ = 1/60`: `h_min ≥ ½ log 30 > 1.70059`, the per-mask error budget `ε_G = 2.5√(2D) ≤ 0.45644`, `h′ = h_min − ε_G > 1.24416`, and the hypothesis `h_min > 2ε_G` holds (`1.70059 > 0.91288`) — so two lemmas apply: **(D1)** every mask reads the dips, `|F(a)| ≥ N·h′` with the orientation read off as `σ_a = −sgn F(a)`; **(D2)** a dominant dip (`β₁ ≥ Σ_{s≠s₁} β_s`) forces `σ` to be the delta at `s₁`. Dip count `k ≤ DN/ψ(½) < 0.108630·N` with `ψ(x) = x log x − x + 1`.

**(iii) The deep-dip trichotomy (Theorem E).** Split the dips at depth `Θ = 5`; shallow mass `Σ_sh < 5N/(60ψ(½)) < 0.543149·N`. If a non-delta sealed law has `D ≤ 1/60`, count its deep dips `m`: `m = 0` contradicts (D1) (`|F(a)| ≤ Σ_sh < 1.24416·N`); `m = 1` gives `β₁ ≥ N·h′ − Σ_sh > 0.701014·N > Σ_sh` — dominance, so (D2) forces delta, contradiction; `m = 2` — on the `N/2` masks with `χ_a(s₁+s₂) = −1`, `|F(a)| ≤ |β₁−β₂| + Σ_sh`, so `β₁ − β₂ > 0.701014·N`, again dominance (`> 0.157865·N` after paying `Σ_sh` twice), contradiction. Hence `m ≥ 3`, and since `ψ` is decreasing on `[0,1]`:

> `N·D ≥ 3ψ(e⁻⁵) = 3(1 − 6e⁻⁵) = 2.87871…`

**(iv) Assembly (Theorem F).** For `n ≤ 5`, the certified computations (§2.3). For `n ≥ 6` and `σ` non-delta: either `D(σ) > 1/60 > 1/63 ≥ 1/(N−1) > D_δ`, or `D(σ) ≤ 1/60` and the trichotomy gives `D(σ) ≥ 2.87871/N > 1/(N−1) > D_δ` (the middle inequality is `2.87871(N−1) > N`, true for all `N ≥ 2`). Both branches strict; the `N` delta orientations share `D_δ` by translation covariance. ∎

The win threshold is `1`, not the sharp runner-up constant: `2.878` clears `N·D_δ < N/(N−1) ≤ 64/63 ≈ 1.016` with a `2.8×` margin — the photo-finish that defeated slack-based routes was an artifact of aiming at `4 log 4 = 5.545`, which remains the open *refinement* (§2.4).
