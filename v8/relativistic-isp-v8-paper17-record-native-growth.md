## 2. The telescope lemma [DERIVED]

**Definition (interval-local action).** An action is *interval-local* if it is a sum over related pairs of a function of the pair's interval **with its induced order**: `S(W) = ОЈ_{x вүә y} f(I(x, y), вүә|_I)`. Paper 11's combined action is of this form: the r-term charges `(r_I вҲ’ ВҪ)ВІ` (a function of the interior's induced comparabilities) on pairs with `|I| вүҘ mвӮҖ`, and the link term charges `Вј` on related pairs with `|I| < mвӮҖ` (a function of interior size).

**Lemma.** Under arrival-only growth вҖ” every arriving element is maximal at arrival, and no relation between already-present elements is ever added or removed вҖ” the interval `I(x, y)` of any related pair is frozen, in membership and in induced order, from the moment its top element `y` lands. Hence any interval-local action telescopes exactly over arrivals: `S(W_T) = ОЈ_t О”S(t)`, where `О”S(t)` is the total charge of the pairs whose top element is the arrival at `t`, computable from the arrival's down-set with its induced order.

*Proof.* (i) *Membership freezes.* No element enters `I(x, y)` after `y` lands: a late arrival `z` would need `z вүә y` вҖ” excluded at `z`'s arrival by maximality and never created afterwards, since every added relation has the arriving element as top; an already-present element `w` would need a new relation `x вүә w` or `w вүә y` among existing elements вҖ” excluded by the same clause. Nothing leaves (relations are never removed). So `I(x, y)`'s membership is fixed once `y` lands. (ii) *No internal rewiring.* The induced order on `I(x, y)` consists of relations among elements present at `y`'s arrival; by hypothesis relations between already-present elements are never modified, so the induced order is fixed too. (iii) *Telescoping.* Each related pair `x вүә y` has its charge `f(I, вүә|_I)` determined at `y`'s arrival and never changed; summing charges by the arrival time of the pair's top element partitions `S(W_T)` exactly. Every pair with top `y` has `x` in `y`'s down-set and `I(x, y)` inside it, so `О”S(t)` is a function of the arrival's down-set with its induced order. вҲҺ

The hypothesis matters: a process whose arrivals are maximal but which also rewires existing relations satisfies "maximal at arrival" and violates the lemma вҖ” arrival-only is the operative clause, and it is exactly note 8's move discipline (moves at seal events; committed relations never revisited). *(Receipt: `u2` CHECK 1 verifies the telescoped total against `j2`'s `action()` verbatim to `1e-6` вҖ” a single-trace spot check (`n = 48`, `ОІ = 0`) of the proved identity, adequate for a lemma but stated as what it is.)*

The consequence is practical and immediate: the misfit-*increment* functional of note 8 В§3(a) is **exact** вҖ” no surrogate was needed вҖ” and each candidate move is priced in `O(|down-set| В· tВІ)` vectorized work. Growth is thereby the *cheap* bench for action-family questions (the abundance-term pricing of В§7 rides exactly this), inverting the expected cost relation with equilibrium search.
