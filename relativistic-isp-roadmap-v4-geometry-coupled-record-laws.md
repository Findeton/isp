## 0. Why The Pivot Is Legitimate

The failure of geometry as a shortcut to the V3 Yang-Mills residual does not
mean geometry is absent from ISP.

It means only this:

$$
\hbox{current finite geometric sources do not hit the specific V3 object }
R\Sigma.
$$

General relativity asks a different first question.  It asks whether finite
hypersurface exchange laws know the spatial metric through the
hypersurface-deformation bracket:

$$
[H[N],H[M]]
=
D[h^{ij}(N\partial_jM-M\partial_jN)].
$$

The V4 pivot is therefore:

$$
\hbox{from "geometry sources Yang-Mills residuals" to "finite ISP curvature
reconstructs fixed-background metric data."}
$$

This is narrower, cleaner, and more Barandes-aligned.

## 1. Barandes Alignment Rule For ISP-GR

$$
\hbox{finite hypersurface kernels first; metric meaning only after exchange
