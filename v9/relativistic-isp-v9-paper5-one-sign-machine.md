## 2. Step (a): the coupled two-species operator

M_A = g²Σ_A(M_A) + g_x Σ_B(M_B), M_B = g²Σ_B(M_B) + g_x Σ_A(M_A),
with Σ the Lüscher-subtracted condensate as a closed eigenvalue sum and g_x the sector's one new dial (|g_x| < g², Hubbard–Stratonovich positivity). Receipt (`ml3b_a_coupled_operator.py`, L = 4/6, dps ≥ 60): the GW identity and Lüscher Ward hold at 1e-121/1e-122 against the 1e-90 pin, **including the coupled-mass breaking identity exactly M_s(γ₅ − s)**; the coupled system solves with M_A, M_B > 0 across g_x ∈ {0, ¼, ½} with the g_x = 0 column reproducing the single-species masses under an independent grid-scan solver (match 1e-40); the cross-susceptibility is 0.632 (secant; local derivative ≈ 0.46, concave). Standing conventions fixed here: zero-mode-subtracted bulk in both gap channels; g_x is a gap-level dial.

## 4. Step (c): the cells, the amendment, and the estimator principle

**The cells.** Under the declared S/P assignment (Γ ∈ {1, γ₅} insertions; the mode import — declared, not derived), the four CHSH cells are windowed connected correlators E_xy with mixed bubbles Π^{a,b}(u, v) = −Tr[Γ_a S(u, v) Γ_b S(v, u)] and exchange through the scalar vertex. The parity is π = sign(E_SS E_SP E_PS E_PP); odd is the Bell-capable class.

**The amendment (pre-receipt, exact).** The original anchor put species A at Q = 0, where the free overlap propagator has no γ₅ component and the mixed bubble reduces to an ε-contraction of a symmetric product — identically zero. Both A-side P-cells die by algebra, not physics. The anchor moved to the parity-breaking pair (Q_A, Q_B) = (1, 2) in an amendment committed before any receipt existed; the demoted (0, 1) row was retained as a measured control and duly printed ~1e-19 against live cells at 1e-2..1e-5. An independent review later verified the identity (≤1.1e-15 pointwise) and adjudicated the amendment could not have been data-tuned.

**The window family and its lesson.** The first protocol (three pinned windows {w = 1, d = 1 shell, d ≤ 2}, two couplings, three-window unanimity) returned WINDOW-CONTINGENT — and, after a review-forced correction of the zero-mode treatment (the joint complex eigenprojector; the receipt's original real-truncated projector was defective and had manufactured four of nine parities), the corrected structure was sharper than noise: **a point-invariant window split** — every contact-dominated window reads even and the contact-free d = 1 shell reads odd, at every evaluable coupling point including a recovered positive root at g_x = −½ (0.109667, 0.503996). No window was crowned (a shell-only pin would have been post-hoc); instead the split forced the principle.

**The estimator principle.** Seals join distinct record threads; a thread coinciding with itself is not a seal; and contact terms are the scheme-dependent part of a two-point function. The two-party object is therefore the **separated** correlator, read window-free at long wavelength by two canonical estimators: E1 = (1/V)Σ_{u≠v} C(u, v) (the p = 0 separated susceptibility) and E2 = the p_min = 2π/L star-averaged separated mode. A constant lattice mode enters E1 at weight (V − 1) and E2 at weight −1 (exact), which later mattered: E1 is maximally sensitive to any long-range residue, E2 maximally insensitive.

## 5. The three adjudications

Each adjudication was pinned with full disclosure of the then-known record, carried a one-shot clause, and refused. Protocols hardened monotonically; no protocol was ever re-run.

**5.1 The naive family (L = 6).** Eight readings — {E1, E2} × {g_x = ¼, ½} × {zero-mode-subtracted, full}: six odd, two even (E1-subtracted at both couplings). ESTIMATOR+CONVENTION-SPLIT. The measured mechanism: the subtraction of the extended topological zero modes injects a long-range rank-one residue into the propagator (the "scar" — the per-distance profile under the subtracted convention plateaus at +3.2e-4 while the full convention decays cleanly to 1e-6; at L = 10 the scar shrinks ×5.5–18, faster than 1/V), and E1 amplifies it ×35 relative to E2 by the weight arithmetic of §4. The deciding cell's convention-delta concentrates exactly there. A registered directional expectation ("odd via d = 1 dominance") was refuted in part and kept.

**5.2 The chirally-consistent family (L = 6).** The improved bilinears ψ̄Γ(1 − D/2)ψ are the covariant densities of the exact lattice chiral symmetry, and they are the operator content the machine's own gap equation already prices — both arguments data-free and available at step (a). The receipt (contraction algebra independently certified: exactly one (1 − D/2) per leg, unambiguous by commutation, match 7e-18) again split 6/2 with the roles rotated: E1 odd on all four legs, E2-subtracted even, all full legs odd. The registered expectation that consistency would narrow the convention fragility was refuted (it widened, 2/4 vs 1/4) and kept. The family closed.

**5.3 The zero-mode resolution and the final run (L = 8/10).** The fork — which treatment of exact topological zero modes is correct for quenched correlators at fixed Q ≠ 0 — was resolved on principle, data-free: the modes are exact, index-verified spectral content of the record operator and carry no deletion license at O(1) mass (M ≈ 0.7–1.06, far from the small-M quenched pathology); the gap-side subtraction is a bulk-condensate *definition* in a scalar equation, licensing nothing about correlators; the S−P combination cancels only the zero-zero piece (chirality algebra, verified in-receipt at 2.2e-32) and does not map onto the four-cell parity; sector-weighting adds imports. The pinned convention: the **full propagator**, with a registered falsifiable discriminator — the sub-vs-full discrepancy must shrink with volume, else any success wears a pinned downgrade. The final protocol: {E1, E2} × {g_x = ¼, ½} × {L = 8, 10}, full convention, improved family, masses re-solved per L. Result: **7/8 odd; the single even leg is E1 at (L = 8, g_x = ¼), where E_SS = +1.769e-05 sits at a genuine zero-crossing** — independently certified 10–12 orders above the numerical noise floor (independent inversion routes agree to 2.4e-19; the cell crosses zero at g_x ≈ 0.279 at L = 8 and the crossing migrates below ¼ by L = 10, where the row is unanimous odd). The dressing pushed the crossing cell positive rather than away from zero: the mechanism that broke tree level relocated rather than resolved. Per the pinned semantics: **FAILS-AT-RPA**; per the route's one-shot clause, the sign question exits the resummed reach of this construction.

## 6. The measurement record, and what it does not license

Against this: the even legs at tree level are, in every certified instance, either subtraction-side readings (the scar mechanism of §5.1) or a genuine near-zero crossing of the dominant cell (§5.3), and at dressed order the crossing relocates into the previously stable legs (§5.4). This is a statement about the *observable's* stability at the accessible orders, not about the underlying branch — which is exactly why the record licenses nothing: a protocol that cannot return unanimity cannot select, and a selection rule relaxed after the fact would un-earn every refusal that came before it.
