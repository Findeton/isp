## Abstract

Paper X established that the gravitational-decoherence *curve* is mechanism-blind: a state-independent sealing process reproduces the Gaussian onset exactly, the resulting channel is always CP-divisible, and the structural axis that would mark a genuinely quantum-gravitational record process is invisible to any reduced-channel measurement. That is an undecidability — a no-go on what the channel certifies. The present companion supplies the matching *positive*, and is careful about what in it is new. The question is whether the record decoherence layer of Paper X can be made relativistic, i.e. whether it respects microcausality. The answer is a conditional inheritance. **What is external, and stated plainly up front:** the all-orders microcausality theorem is the 2025 Gundhi–Diósi–Carlesso (GDC) result for a collapse model driven by a Lorentz-invariant positive-type colored noise; the obstruction at the objective-statevector layer is Haag's theorem / unitary inequivalence of interacting representations (Torre–Varadarajan for the failure of functional Tomonaga–Schwinger evolution above two dimensions). Neither is a contribution of this note. **What is program-internal:** the observation — *about the records* — that the seal is the right kind of object for the GDC hypothesis. The record seal is an algebraically self-adjoint weight-0 scalar (`E = E² = E*`, content `χ` a relabel-invariant KL number), and its locality kernel is, by construction, a function of the substrate's interval invariant alone, positive-type by Bochner (finite-sample Gram certificate `+2.78 × 10⁻⁹`) with quartic ultraviolet decay — exactly the Lorentz-invariant colored correlator the GDC theorem needs. Consequently the decoherence layer *inherits* GDC microcausality if and only if the holonomy diamond coarse-grains, under an emergent Lorentz action, to a *point-local* scalar seal density (collar → 0). On a finite causal lattice the inheritance hinge is operational: a point-local scalar seal commutes with every spacelike probe to all orders tested, while a region-smeared seal that straddles a spacelike gap breaks microcausality already at first order. This reduces the parent's vague "covariance frontier" to **one sharp, named, borrowable emergence premise** — point-locality — and is therefore a *conditional* positive companion to the parent's undecidability, not a covariant-collapse theorem. Two riders: the colored (not white) character of the noise is structural-by-construction, because the records' discreteness supplies the ultraviolet cutoff that the literature imposes by hand to avoid infinite heating; and the objective interacting-field statevector remains the Haag wall that blocks every objective-collapse program equally — SHARD is no worse off there, and stays strictly stochastic-linear to avoid the deterministic-nonlinear signalling no-go. Receipts: `c1_lorentz_scalar_seal_mcc.py`, `c2_derived_noise_kernel.py`.

## 1. What is external and what is internal

This note has two external pillars and one internal observation, and the honesty of the result depends on keeping them apart.

**External (pillar 1): the microcausality theorem.** Gundhi, Diósi and Carlesso (2025) proved that a relativistic dynamical-collapse model whose reduction operator is a point-local self-adjoint Lorentz scalar, driven by a Lorentz-invariant positive-type colored noise, satisfies microcausality to all orders — spacelike-separated operations commute, with a finite collapse-induced heating rate. The theorem is theirs. This companion does not re-derive it and claims no improvement on it.

**External (pillar 2): the statevector obstruction.** A frame-independent, objectively collapsing statevector for an interacting field with variable particle number requires unitarily equivalent Hilbert spaces across Cauchy surfaces. Generic interacting QFT lacks this: it is the Haag obstruction (unitary inequivalence of interacting representations), and the failure of functional Tomonaga–Schwinger evolution between arbitrary Cauchy surfaces above two dimensions is the Torre–Varadarajan result. This wall is long known and is not this note's discovery.

**Internal (the contribution): a statement about the records.** The program-internal content is *not* a new theorem of mainstream physics. It is a positioning of the SHARD record seal against the two external results: the record seal is the *right kind of object* for the GDC hypothesis — a self-adjoint scalar carrying a Lorentz-invariant positive-type kernel — so the open covariance surface of Paper X collapses to a single sharp emergence premise (point-locality), conditional on which the decoherence layer borrows GDC's microcausality. The result is therefore a *conditional* — an inheritance gated by one named, unproved premise — and a *positioning* of the seal against a published theorem. It is written that way throughout, and it is the positive complement to Paper X's negative (undecidability) verdict: the parent says the channel cannot certify the mechanism; this note says the channel layer can nonetheless be made relativistic on one borrowable hinge.

The discipline is pre-geometric. The seal, its content, and its kernel are record-internal objects (a projection, a KL number, a function of the substrate's own interval invariant). Spacetime and the Lorentz group enter only as the *emergent* structure the records are tested against; the causal order against which microcausality is stated is the frame-invariant order of the record substrate, and the discreteness that supplies the ultraviolet cutoff is the causal-set / flash structure of the substrate.

## 2. The seal is the right kind of object: a self-adjoint scalar with a Lorentz-invariant positive-type kernel

The GDC hypothesis has two non-trivial ingredients, and the records supply both at the algebraic / construction level.

**The seal is a self-adjoint scalar.** Receipt `c1_lorentz_scalar_seal_mcc.py` confirms (sympy-exact) that the seal `E` is an orthogonal projection, `E = E² = E*`, so the reflection `q = 2E − 1` is a self-adjoint involution — a scalar seal — and the content `χ = D(P_AB ‖ P_BA)` is a weight-0 KL *number*, invariant under the substrate's internal relabelings. This is the same self-adjoint-scalar character as the GDC reduction operator `Q = ½ α :φ²:`. The caveat, carried forward as the §3 premise, is explicit: "scalar" here is established under the substrate's *internal* automorphisms; whether it is a scalar under the *emergent* Lorentz action is an emergence statement, not an algebraic fact.

**The kernel is Lorentz-invariant, positive-type, and colored.** The GDC noise condition is that the collapse field be driven by a Lorentz-invariant colored correlator `G((x − y)²)` — a function of the interval alone — with finite ultraviolet energy. The record locality kernel `f(s²)` is, by construction, a function of the substrate's invariant interval `s²` alone, with quartic ultraviolet decay; this is a Tier-B (emergent-geometry) statement, not a Tier-A output. It is moreover *positive-type*: receipt `c1` returns a finite-sample Gram minimum eigenvalue `+2.77752268119 × 10⁻⁹ ≥ 0`, a numerical certificate of positivity that is *exact* by Bochner (the spectrum `exp(−q⁴)` is non-negative), not an intrinsic positivity margin. So the noise condition GDC requires — a Lorentz-invariant colored `G((x − y)²)` of finite energy — is met at Tier B by the record kernel as it stands. Positivity-of-type is SHARD's reasonable strengthening of the bare GDC requirement, not a GDC condition.

The honest scope: only the microcausality half of GDC is inherited. The GDC *finite-energy* half additionally requires their normal-ordering prescription, which the records do not carry; this note borrows the microcausality half alone.

## 3. The inheritance is conditional on one sharp premise: point-locality

With the scalar seal and the Lorentz-invariant positive-type kernel in hand, the GDC all-orders recursion needs one further input: the collapse-driving density must be **point-local**. This is exactly where the record primitive differs from the theorem's hypothesis. The record primitive is a finite holonomy *diamond* — a region with a collar — not a point. Locality is therefore the one *new* load-bearing gap; the emergent-Lorentz-scalar property of §2 is the pre-existing covariance-frontier premise, carried forward, not a fresh assumption.

Receipt `c1_lorentz_scalar_seal_mcc.py` makes the inheritance condition operational on a finite causal-lattice operator model, with sites carrying a discrete causal partial order (a lattice light-cone mimicking the emergent causal set). A **point-local** scalar seal density acts at a single site (a single-site self-adjoint operator) — the collar → 0 limit. A **region-smeared** seal density acts on a cluster of sites that can straddle a spacelike gap — the diamond with finite collar. Against a spacelike-separated probe `O(z)`:

- the **point-local** scalar seal commutes with `O(z)`, and the nested (GDC-style) commutator **vanishes at all orders 1–5 tested** — microcausality holds;
- the **region-smeared** seal **fails** microcausality already at first order — the finite collar straddles the spacelike gap and the commutator does not vanish.

This operationalizes the **locality ⟺ spacelike-commutation hinge** on which the GDC recursion turns. It is not a re-derivation of that recursion: for a single point-local scalar density the higher orders vanish trivially once the first does, and GDC's published all-orders result — nesting distinct multi-point operators against two probes — is what is borrowed. With the hinge met, the decoherence layer **inherits** GDC's all-orders microcausality, and with it the spacelike half `[H(x), H(y)] = 0` of Tomonaga–Schwinger integrability, **if and only if** the holonomy diamond coarse-grains, under an emergent Lorentz action, to a **point-local self-adjoint scalar seal density** (collar → 0).

This is the note's central move, and its honest size. It does not solve "the covariance problem"; it converts the parent's open covariance frontier into **one named, motivated, unproved emergence premise** — point-locality — with a clean failure mode (region-smearing breaks microcausality at first order). The premise is motivated: a coarse-graining that produces a relativistic continuum should send a finite microstructure to a point, and the causal-set / flash discreteness covariantizes the kinematics in just this way. But it is an emergence statement about the diamond → point limit, not a theorem, and the inheritance is conditional on it. Reducing an open frontier to a single load-bearing hypothesis with a clean failure mode is a genuine narrowing of the open surface, and is all that is claimed.

## 4. The colored noise is structural, not derived

It is tempting to claim more — that the records *derive* the GDC kernel, beating the literature's free functional choice. Receipt `c2_derived_noise_kernel.py` runs the decisive test and the answer is no, on two independent counts. First, the natural Tier-A object — the odometer content `χ` — is a weight-0 scalar KL *number*, the wrong *type* to be a two-point correlation function `G(s²)`; it cannot even be evaluated on the kernel's spacetime-interval domain without importing the very emergent geometry under test. Second, the positive-type quartic template `ĝ(q) = exp(−q⁴/β⁴)` is one admissible profile among many (the receipt exhibits three distinct positive-type, finite-energy kernels sharing any one cutoff scale); like the literature's kernel it is *free* within the positive-type, finite-energy class. So the kernel is not derived; it has the same "one of many" status the literature's kernel has.

What the records *do* supply is structural, and smaller, and worth stating exactly. The literature imposes a *colored* (non-white) kernel by hand precisely to avoid an ultraviolet catastrophe: white noise (`ĝ ≡ 1`) makes the heating integral `∫ q² ĝ(q) dq` diverge — infinite energy injection. Receipt `c2` records this: the white integral diverges, while the quartic kernel gives a finite

  `∫₀^∞ q² e^{−q⁴} dq = Γ(3/4)/4 = 0.30635417561629441128…`

(mpmath dps = 40, matching the closed form to all printed digits). In SHARD the coloring is *not* imposed: the records' principled discreteness gives a built-in ultraviolet cutoff, so the noise being **colored rather than white is forced by construction**, and the infinite-heating catastrophe — the single hardest *ad hoc* repair in the collapse literature — cannot arise. The honest claim is therefore "the *coloring* of the noise is structural" — a real, smaller positive result — and **not** "the kernel is derived." Discreteness fixes the scale, not the functional shape.

## 5. The objective interacting-field statevector is the field's shared wall

The decoherence layer (§§2–4) is the layer this note can move. The objective *statevector* layer is the harder obstruction, and it is not the records' to remove. A frame-independent, objectively collapsing statevector for an **interacting** field with variable particle number requires a consistent collapsing wavefunctional across all Cauchy surfaces, hence unitarily equivalent Hilbert spaces on different surfaces. Generic interacting QFT lacks this — the **Haag obstruction** (unitary inequivalence of interacting representations), with the failure of functional Tomonaga–Schwinger evolution above two dimensions established by Torre–Varadarajan. This wall is external and long known. Fedida (2025) is instructive precisely here: it *assumes* unitarily equivalent Hilbert spaces along Cauchy surfaces and from them derives positive Einstein-causality results (spacelike commutation, no-signalling) — corroborating the §3 spacelike-microcausality side — while acknowledging that the precondition fails generically. It does not remove the wall.

The residue factors cleanly. Write the remaining obstruction as `B = B₁ × B₂`, where `B₁` asks whether an interaction-local Tomonaga–Schwinger field unitary exists at all and `B₂` asks whether collapse can be inserted given `B₁`. Everything except `B₁` is discharged by §§2–4 — `B₂` modulo the same §3 point-local premise that conditions the microcausality inheritance. So the two-layer status is clean: `B₁` is the **unconditional shared Haag wall**, and `B₂` holds **conditional on the one premise**. SHARD is therefore no worse off than the rest of the field at the statevector layer — the wall blocks relativistic GRW-flash, CSL, and every objective-statevector collapse program equally — and strictly better off at the decoherence layer, where it supplies the scalar seal and the colored kernel that the generic model must postulate.

Two disciplines close the scope. SHARD stays strictly **stochastic-linear**: the records' nonlinearity lives entirely in the *stochastic* reduction, not in a deterministic nonlinear term, which would fall foul of the deterministic-nonlinear signalling no-go. And the beable claim is scoped to what the substrate carries — a relativistic flash / event ontology (the seals are the flashes), for which covariant constructions are known in the free / distinguishable case (Tumulka) — not an objective interacting-field wavefunctional, which runs into `B₁`.

## 6. What this companion does and does not claim

**Claims (each a statement about the records, not a new mainstream theorem).** (1) The record seal is a self-adjoint weight-0 scalar and its Tier-B kernel is a Lorentz-invariant positive-type colored correlator (Gram certificate `+2.78 × 10⁻⁹`, positive by Bochner) — the two non-trivial GDC ingredients, *supplied* by the records at the algebraic / construction level. (2) The decoherence layer *inherits* GDC all-orders microcausality, and the spacelike half of Tomonaga–Schwinger integrability, *if and only if* the holonomy diamond coarse-grains to a point-local scalar seal density (point-local seals commute with spacelike probes to all orders 1–5 tested; region-smeared seals fail at first order) — a *conditional* on one named emergence premise. (3) The noise's being *colored* is structural-by-construction (discreteness supplies the cutoff the literature imposes by hand), *not* a derivation of the kernel. (4) The objective interacting-field statevector is the field's shared Haag wall, blocking SHARD no more than Tumulka / CSL.

**Non-claims.** This note does **not** claim a covariant collapse theory, nor an objective relativistic interacting-field statevector — that is the shared wall. It does **not** claim to derive the noise kernel — only that the coloring is structural. It does **not** claim the point-local emergence premise as a theorem; it is a motivated, named, unproved hypothesis, and the microcausality inheritance is conditional on it. The microcausality theorem is **external** (GDC 2025); the statevector wall is the **external** Haag / Torre–Varadarajan obstruction, not Fedida's (Fedida derives positive results *assuming* the equivalence, and corroborates only the spacelike-microcausality side). No metric or Lorentz structure is used as a derivation input. The contribution is exactly the conditional positioning of the records against these two external results, and it is the positive companion to Paper X's undecidability no-go.
