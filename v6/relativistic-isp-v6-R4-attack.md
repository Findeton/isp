# R4 / Gravity — interacting Tomonaga–Schwinger integrability: attack + verdict (2026-06-14)

R4 is the residue the user audited directly (papers 1, 2 read in full this session). For a Clay-hard
residue the honest "attack" is: state the barrier precisely, confirm it is the *known* hard problem

## The precise residue
SHARD's entire gravity program (Program A, causal-set) reduces — provably, by papers 1–2 — to ONE object:

> **(R4) Interacting TS integrability.** Does the *interacting* indivisible (ISP) reconstruction admit a
> division-event process whose memory is a **Lorentz-invariant, microcausal correlation `f(s²)`** on the
> division-event causal set — i.e. is the indivisible interacting evolution **Tomonaga–Schwinger
> integrable**, `[𝓗(x),𝓗(y)]=0` for spacelike `x,y` — consistent with (i) standard microcausal
> interacting QFT as its first-order content and (ii) the division events sourcing gravity?
> Plus the **A/B fork**: is `f(s²)` ISP-**derived** (branch A) or **postulated** (branch B)?

By HKT/Jacobson (papers 1 §1.2), *if* this closes, GR follows. So R4 is the whole ballgame for gravity.

## This IS the known-hard problem (not a SHARD-specific gap)
- The **free** case exists (Tumulka rGRWf 2006, manifestly Lorentz-invariant flash process).
- The **interacting** case is the long-open problem of relativistic objective-collapse models. Decisively:
  Tumulka's interacting flash model (2020) **takes a microcausal TS evolution as INPUT** — it does not
  construct one. *Constructing* it is exactly R4, and it is open across the entire field (Pearle's
  energy divergence, Bedingham's mediating field, Adler–Bassi, the 2025 arXiv:2507.06954 recipe all
  circle it). So R4 is a recognized frontier of mathematical physics, not a fixable SHARD oversight.

## What SHARD adds, and where it lands
- **The indivisibility lever:** non-Markovianity = intrinsic memory = a candidate `f(s²)`. Paper 1 §6 shows
  a covariant `f(s²)` makes the interacting heating **finite and frame-independent** (necessary condition,
  on a *model* correlation), and paper 2 §4 pins the admissible class to **quartic-damped** `ĝ(q²)=e^{−q⁴/β⁴}`
  (the covariant Gaussian blows up spacelike — a genuine correction).
- **But the concrete construction is branch B.** Porting the 2025 recipe (`:φ̂²:` + normal-ordering +
  quartic memory) yields a **covariant collapse MODEL** with free parameters `(γ, β)` and `∝N` energy
  production — markers of a *modification*, not a reconstruction-derivation. Paper 2 §5 states the A/B
  fork as **theorem TARGETS** (A-Form, A-Scale), i.e. conjectures, not results.

## Tractable sub-pieces? (honest scan)
1. **Microcausality of the ported dissipator** `[Q̂(x),Q̂(y)]=0` spacelike for `Q̂=½α:φ̂²:` — *checkable*
   (locality + normal-ordering; the 2025 paper claims it). Verifying it for the ICS version would confirm
   branch B is *internally consistent* (a covariant collapse model exists) — but that **confirms the
   import, it does not close R4** (which is branch A: is the memory *derived*?).
2. **The A-Form theorem target** (prove `f(s²)`'s invariant form/falloff/scale are forced by covariance +
   TS-integrability + no-vacuum-record + finite stress-energy) — this **is** the open analytic problem;
   no numerical sub-step reaches it.
3. **The Q3 obstruction** (a covariant localization `dp/2ω` is non-sharp by **Hegerfeldt**, in tension with
   a point-localized gravity source; a mass-localizing flash is effectively state-dependent, risking the
   nonlinear-collapse TS no-go, arXiv:2511.15935) — this may be a genuine **structural obstruction**, not
   just an open step. It is the sharpest internal tension and the most likely place R4 could turn out
   *negative*.

## Verdict
**R4 is genuinely Clay-hard / analytic — the open relativistic-collapse-integrability problem.** SHARD's
contribution is real but is *mapping + a branch-B candidate*: it reduces all of gravity to this one
residue, identifies the indivisibility lever, pins the memory class (quartic), and clears the
energy-finiteness facet on a model — while the **derivation (branch A) is open and the Q3 tension may be
a structural obstruction.** No tractable advance closes it; the one decidable sub-piece (dissipator
microcausality) only certifies the import, not the derivation. Honest status: **not closable with
available methods; a frontier, with a live possibility (Q3) that it is obstructed rather than merely open.**
