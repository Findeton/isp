## Abstract

We develop the gravity sector of the indivisible-record (ISP) program along its
foliation-invariant substrate. The primitive is the network of **division events** — the
records at which an indivisible stochastic process momentarily factorizes — whose causal
order is Lorentz-invariant and whose counting fixes volume, so that "order + number =
geometry" promotes the division-event causal set to the geometric substrate. On this
substrate the *intended* dynamics is the realization of the
hypersurface-deformation (Dirac–Schwinger) algebra: *if* that algebra is realized on a

(`S` proportional to horizon area) is **not** supplied, so that route is not completed. We show that the program reduces to **two coupled residues**: whether the

## 1. The proposal

An indivisible stochastic process (ISP) reconstructs quantum mechanics over a definite
configuration space with a transition law that does not factorize through intermediate
times,
```math
\Gamma(t_2,t_0) \neq \Gamma(t_2,t_1)\,\Gamma(t_1,t_0),
```
except at isolated **division events**, where the process momentarily factorizes
(Chapman–Kolmogorov holds) — the loci of record formation. The continuous beable history
has no consistent joint distribution across foliations; this is the obstruction that a
metric laid on that history would inherit, and the reason a continuum-beable route to
geometry over-determines (as it does for covariant Bohmian field theory).

The division-event layer evades that obstruction. A division event is an **objective,
frame-independent** occurrence — all foliations agree a record formed — and the **causal
order** among division events is Lorentz-invariant (timelike order is frame-independent;
spacelike pairs are unordered, which a causal set permits). So the division events form a
causal set `(C, ≺)`, the natural foliation-invariant object, on which the geometry is
reconstructed:

- **Kinematics — order + number = geometry [EST, Sorkin].** The order `≺` recovers the
  conformal (light-cone) structure; the event count recovers the volume; together they fix
  the metric up to the discreteness scale.
- **Dynamics — the hypersurface-deformation algebra on antichains.** A spatial slice is a
  maximal antichain of mutually unrelated events — a foliation-invariant combinatorial
  object. Its deformations are the discrete analogue of hypersurface deformations, and
  their algebra is the Dirac–Schwinger algebra. By the **Hojman–Kuchař–Teitelboim**
  theorem, the algebra's realization is *essentially unique* and is general relativity under
  the theorem's hypotheses — the dynamical variable being the spatial metric with its
  conjugate momentum, two-derivative (second-order) dynamics, ultralocality, and an
  inverse-metric structure function (fixed only up to the Newton constant `G` and a
  cosmological constant `Λ`, which the algebra leaves undetermined). That the
  antichain-deformation algebra is *realized
  faithfully* on such a metric-plus-momentum phase space on this substrate is, however, not
  established here (Section 3 exhibits only the locality skeleton and a non-discriminating
  finite-sprinkling drift); HKT uniqueness is invoked as the target, not a reached conclusion.
  Equivalently, **if** division-event counting across a
  local causal horizon supplies the entropy, **Jacobson's**
  thermodynamic derivation returns the Einstein equation of state from `δQ = T δS`.

ISP's intended contribution would be to **derive the sprinkling law** (the record
rate) that causal-set theory must otherwise postulate; whether it does is the open
substrate-Lorentz-invariance residue. The program therefore reduces to **two coupled
residues** — whether the interacting reconstruction is Tomonaga–Schwinger integrable
(Sections 4–6) and whether the substrate is forced to sprinkle Lorentz-invariantly (the
residue of Section 2) — plus the live Hegerfeldt obstruction (Section 6).

## 2. The kinematic leg: the division rate is a Lorentz scalar

The substrate is Lorentz-invariant iff the division-event point process is **Poisson per
proper 4-volume** — equivalently, iff the division *rate* is a Lorentz scalar (per proper
time), not a per-coordinate-time rate. This is a sharp, operational condition.

**Kill-test [PROBE].** A frame-reconstruction estimator (scan trial boosts; the Fano-factor
β-dependence of slab counts) is **consistent with** a Poisson-per-4-volume process (no
recoverable preferred frame) and **separates** it from any synchronized or regularly-clocked
global-time process at the illustrative central values (the rest frame is recovered at signal
`S ≈ 77` versus a Poisson null `S ≈ 0.21` — a single finite-sprinkling receipt, no error bars,
full provenance in the computational companion, not an error-barred discriminant). On an
accelerated worldline, a per-proper-time rate gives the *same* division count in every
frame (Lorentz-invariant), while a per-coordinate-time rate is frame-dependent.

**The rate is scalar for interacting fields [PROBE + ARG].** For a record register linearly
coupled to a scalar field (an Unruh–DeWitt detector, "click = record = division event"):
the proper acceleration is boost-invariant; the pulled-back vacuum two-point function is
stationary and thermal, `Δt² − Δx² = (4/a²)\sinh²(aΔτ/2)`, and KMS-periodic at the Unruh
temperature `T = a/2π`; an inertial detector does not flash in any frame. The scalar rate
**survives arbitrary self-interactions**: by the Källén–Lehmann representation the
interacting correlation is a positive superposition of free ones, `W_{int} = ∫ ρ(μ²)
W_{free}`, so stationarity (scalar rate) and KMS-thermality are carried by Lorentz
invariance, not by freeness. Only a non-covariant (preferred-frame) coupling breaks it.

**The two legs share a temperature.** The flash rate's Unruh temperature `T = a/2π` supplies
the temperature in Jacobson's `δQ = T δS`; the entropy/area side `δS` is not supplied here.
The kinematic leg (a scalar division rate) and the temperature entering the dynamic leg are
therefore the same structure read two ways.

## 3. The dynamics leg: the metric and the structure function from causal data

Each [PROBE] number below is a receipt from finite causal-set sprinklings (the sprinkling
dimension, event count, density, estimator, and statistic uncertainty are given with the
reproduction in the computational companion); we quote the central values here.

The Dirac–Schwinger algebra's realization needs three ingredients on the causal set — the
spatial metric (structure function `g^{ij}`), the lapse gradient `∂N`, and an oriented
spatial direction — all of which are present in the data.

- **Dimension and metric [PROBE].** The causal order recovers the dimension (e.g. `d = 2,
  3, 4` from order statistics to a few percent). The metric is extractable from order +
  number: interval cardinality gives the Lorentzian volume, longest chains give proper
  time, and a least-squares fit recovers the metric tensor — flat sprinklings return
  Minkowski to a few percent, and curved sprinklings show a sign-resolved curvature signal.
- **The hypersurface-deformation algebra [PROBE].** Antichain deformations carry the
  locality skeleton of the Dirac–Schwinger algebra (spacelike-separated moves commute;
  timelike pairs are sequenced by causal order). The general-relativistic content is the
  **tangential flow-drift**: the commutator of two normal deformations drags the slice
  labeling by `ξ = g^{ij}(N∂_jM − M∂_jN)`. On the recovered spatial frame this drift tracks
  the general-relativistic profile `ξ`: a positive but modest correlation (`+0.63`) on finite
  Lorentzian sprinklings — consistent with the GR profile within the discreteness noise, not
  an error-barred discriminant — while the noise-free grid mechanism is exact (`+1.00`).
- **Consistent with general relativity, not Hořava [PROBE].** In curved space the flow-drift *coefficient*
  recovered from order + number is the **curved inverse metric** `g^{ij}` (correlation
  `+0.99` against `1/Ω²`, dipping with curvature — an illustrative finite-sprinkling receipt,
  not an error-barred discriminant), *not a constant* — and this
  field-dependent `g^{ij}` is the load-bearing structural *signature* that, **if** the
  sprinkling is Lorentzian, makes the encoded structure function field-dependent (GR-type)
  rather than constant (preferred-foliation-type); proper-time (chains) and proper-volume
  (counts) agree (isotropic, `z = 1`), so the
  encoded geometry is the isotropic GR structure function rather than an anisotropic (`z > 1`)
  Lifshitz one — a property of the assumed proper-uniform sprinkling, not a dynamical exclusion
  of Hořava gravity. The full spatial-metric *tensor* is recovered in `2+1` and the physical
  `3+1` (the drift vector rotates by an angle consistent with the tensor's prediction within
  the discreteness noise, excluding an isotropic/scalar coefficient). **Scope:** this shows the *construction* is general-relativity-faithful in
  curved space — order + number deliver the curved metric as the structure function —
  **conditional on a Lorentzian (proper-uniform) sprinkling**. The causal set faithfully
  encodes whatever is sprinkled, so "is the substrate forced to be such a sprinkling"
  reduces to the substrate-Lorentz-invariance residue of Section 2, shared with the rate leg.

## 4. The covariance residue

Granting a Lorentz-invariant substrate (the residue of Section 2), the dynamics leg reduces
to one object: does the *interacting* indivisible reconstruction
stay Lorentz-covariant — equivalently, is it **Tomonaga–Schwinger integrable**
(`[𝓗(x), 𝓗(y)] = 0` for spacelike `x, y`), carrying a Lorentz-invariant, microcausal record
correlation `f(s²)` on the division-event set?

The free case exists: Tumulka's relativistic flash process is a manifestly Lorentz-invariant
division-event substrate for non-interacting systems. The interacting case is the
long-standing open problem of relativistic objective-collapse models: the interacting flash
construction takes a microcausal Tomonaga–Schwinger evolution as **input**; constructing one
is exactly this residue.

**Energy-finiteness is downstream of covariance [PROBE].** The sharpest concrete obstacle is
the relativistic-collapse energy-production divergence: white (Markovian) record noise heats
the field at a UV-divergent rate. Indivisibility *is* non-Markovianity — a finite memory.
The decisive requirement is covariance: a Lorentz-invariant interval correlation `f(s²)`.
Along any inertial worldline `s²` is the proper time squared, so `f(s²)` is automatically
frame-independent, and covariance then makes the heating *identical* in every frame (a
frame-fixed memory, by contrast, heats frame-dependently). Finiteness is a separate condition:
the UV (short-interval) heating rate is controlled by the two-sided decay of the spectrum
`ĝ(q²)`, which must decay in the **spacelike** region; the admissible class is therefore
a positive-type interval correlation with two-sided ultraviolet decay — positive-type because
its spectrum `ĝ(q²) = e^{−q⁴/β⁴}` is non-negative everywhere (the position-space kernel `f`
itself need not be sign-definite), the quartic-damped form being the worked template. So smoothness and covariance are one object: a
Lorentz-invariant, microcausal indivisible correlation, with energy-finiteness as the
expected consequence at template level (the quartic-damped kernel; a finite stress-energy for
the full interacting field is a residue, not a theorem here).

## 5. The Branch-A/B status: the memory is postulated, not derived

We state the status of the concrete construction plainly. The covariant record process that
realizes the above is built from a **quadratic local record operator** `Q̂ = ½α:φ̂²:` (a
Lorentz scalar whose non-relativistic limit is the mass-density operator), a non-Markovian
**Lorentz-invariant** correlation `f(s²)` of the admissible quartic-damped class, and a
**normal-ordering** prescription (which removes vacuum record production — no records form
from the inertial vacuum, matching Section 2). Microcausality follows from the locality of
`Q̂` and the time-ordering of the double-commutator dissipator.

This is a **covariant collapse model**: it modifies quantum mechanics (a dissipator with a
free rate `γ`, genuine decoherence, energy production `∝ N`) and carries **free parameters**
`(γ, β)`. The record memory `f(s²)` is **selected as an admissible covariant kernel, not
derived from the indivisible reconstruction**. This is the honest **Branch B**: a covariant
record-collapse model whose flashes are the division events and source semiclassical
gravity, with indivisibility supplying the non-Markovian covariant memory — coherent and
concrete, but weaker than a derivation of gravity from a pure ISP reconstruction.

**Branch A** — the strong route — would *derive* `f(s²)`: its invariant form, spectral
falloff, and scale fixed by the record law itself, under covariance, Tomonaga–Schwinger
integrability, no-vacuum-record normalization, and finite stress-energy. That is the open
derivation target; the free `(γ, β)` and the `∝ N` energy production are the markers that
the present construction has not reached it.

## 6. The structural obstruction (Q3)

A live obstruction may make Branch A unreachable, not merely unproven. Newton–Wigner
localization is sharp in a given frame but **non-covariant** (frame-dependent); any
**covariant** (frame-independent) localization of positive-energy states — built on the
Lorentz-invariant measure `dp/2ω` — is **not sharply local**: covariantly-localized states
carry superluminal tails (Hegerfeldt's theorem). The gravity sector wants division events
localized *enough* to source gravity at a point. So the open question is whether the records
can be covariantly localized yet localized enough to source gravity — the smooth-versus-sharp
tension that runs through the entire relativistic-flash program. Sharpened further: a
physical, mass-localizing flash is effectively a state-dependent event, and state-dependent
modifications break Tomonaga–Schwinger integrability at spacelike separation
(the nonlinear-collapse no-go). ISP is a *linear* stochastic reconstruction — linear in the
*unconditional* stochastic master equation — so it aims past that no-go at that level. But
whether the Section 5 gravity-sourcing coupling `Q̂ = ½α :φ²:` reintroduces an effective
spacelike state-dependence (which would re-trigger the no-go) is itself part of the open Q3
residue, not settled here: the "linear reconstruction" framing does not by itself make the
Branch-B model no-go-safe. This is the sharpest internal tension and the most likely place
the residue is obstructed rather than merely open.

## 7. What this paper does not claim

It does not derive general relativity; it does not solve the relativistic interacting-QFT
reconstruction; it does not derive a graviton or a holographic area law; it does not show
that ISP's division events Poisson-sprinkle. The Section 3 curved-coefficient result shows
the *construction* is general-relativity-faithful **conditional on a Lorentzian sprinkling**;
it does not show the *dynamics* selects an isotropic (general-relativistic) substrate — that
is the substrate-Lorentz-invariance residue. The concrete realization is **Branch B** (a
covariant collapse model with free parameters); whether the memory is forced (Branch A) is
open, with a live structural obstruction (Section 6). The fallback, if full general
relativity is out of reach on this substrate, is **unimodular** sourcing by the
non-conserved record matter, with one conjecture: that unimodularity's fixed volume form is
the geometric shadow of the indivisible process's conserved probability measure.
